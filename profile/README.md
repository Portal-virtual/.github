# Portal — Virtual Landscapes for Focus and Relaxation With Smart Light Sync

## About Portal

Portal provides immersive virtual landscapes designed to boost focus, reduce stress, and enhance relaxation — displayed on your screen while synchronizing with smart lighting. Portal combines 4K dynamic scenes (forests, mountains, oceans, space, city views, abstract art), ambient soundscapes (rain, waves, wind, birds, white noise, lo-fi), and smart light integration (Philips Hue, Nanoleaf, LIFX, IKEA Tradfri). Portal enables users to transform any room into a calming environment — matching room lighting colors to the on-screen scene in real time. Portal works as a desktop app (menu bar or full screen) and includes focus timers (Pomodoro), breathing exercises, and ambient mode. Portal is trusted by remote workers, students, creative professionals, and anyone seeking digital wellness.

## Deep Dive — Virtual Landscape Smart Sync Notes

Scene library — 50+ dynamic landscapes, 4K resolution, animated elements (clouds moving, water rippling, leaves falling)  
Smart light integration — Philips Hue, Nanoleaf, LIFX, IKEA Tradfri, Belkin WeMo, TP-Link Kasa  
Color sync modes — average color of entire scene, dominant colors, animated transitions matching scene changes  
Soundscapes — built-in library, upload custom audio, volume mixing, nature sounds + lo-fi + binaural beats  
Focus tools — Pomodoro timer (25/5), focus session tracker, break reminders, full-screen immersion
